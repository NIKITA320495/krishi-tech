About us

From Seed to Sale: Revolutionizing Farming with Data-Driven Decisions.

KrishiTech is dedicated to empowering farmers with advanced agricultural solutions and expert advice. Our platform connects farmers with cutting-edge technology, sustainable farming practices, and a wealth of resources to enhance productivity and sustainability. We strive to revolutionize agriculture by providing tools and information that help farmers make informed decisions, increase yields, and promote environmental stewardship. At KrishiTech, we believe in the power of innovation and community. Our mission is to support farmers by offering personalized recommendations, real-time updates, and a collaborative space for sharing knowledge and experiences. Whether it's through our blogs, chatbot assistance, or interactive forums, we aim to create a thriving agricultural ecosystem that benefits farmers and the environment alike. Join us in our journey to cultivate a prosperous and sustainable future for agriculture.

KrishiTech was founded and is owned by a team of passionate second-year engineering students: Nikita Babbar, Manya Joshi, Ritwick Johari, and Piyush Singh. They are pursuing B.Tech in Artificial Intelligence & Data Science from the University School of Automation & Robotics. Their dedication and expertise drive KrishiTech's mission to transform the farming landscape through innovation and community support.

Services

Crop Prediction

At KrishiTech, our Crop Recommendation service, leverages advanced AI and data analytics to suggest the best crops for farmers to plant. By analyzing historical data, weather patterns, and soil conditions, we help farmers choose the most suitable crops to optimize yields and maximize productivity. Our insights empower farmers to make informed decisions, reduce risks, and improve overall farm efficiency, ensuring a prosperous harvest season.

Fertilizer Prediction

Our Fertilizer Recommendation service, provides personalized fertilizer recommendations based on soil health and crop requirements. By utilizing soil testing data and AI-driven algorithms, we offer precise fertilizer plans that enhance soil fertility and promote healthy crop growth. This service helps farmers optimize fertilizer use, reduce costs, and minimize environmental impact, ensuring sustainable and productive farming practices.

  • Learn More

Disease Prediction

KrishiTech's Disease Prediction service, uses cutting-edge technology to identify and predict crop diseases before they spread. By analyzing environmental factors, plant health data, and historical disease occurrences, we provide early warnings and actionable insights to farmers. This proactive approach helps in timely intervention, reducing crop loss, and maintaining healthy yields, ultimately safeguarding the farmer's livelihood and the agricultural ecosystem.

What We Do?

At KrishiTech, we empower farmers with innovative agricultural solutions and expert advice to enhance productivity and sustainability. Our platform offers advanced tools, sustainable farming practices, and real-time updates, providing personalized recommendations and a wealth of resources. Our Crop Recommendation service leverages AI and data analytics to suggest optimal crops by analyzing historical data, weather patterns, and soil conditions, helping farmers make informed decisions and maximize yields. The Fertilizer Prediction service provides precise, personalized fertilizer plans based on soil health and crop requirements, optimizing fertilizer use, reducing costs, and minimizing environmental impact. Our Disease Prediction service uses cutting-edge technology to identify and predict crop diseases early, offering actionable insights and early warnings to prevent disease spread and reduce crop loss. By fostering a collaborative community and promoting environmental stewardship, we aim to revolutionize agriculture for a prosperous and sustainable future.

Our Products

KrishiTech provides a comprehensive suite of agricultural products and services designed to empower farmers at every stage. Our offerings include cutting-edge crop recommendations, precise fertilizer suggestions, and advanced disease prediction using AI and data analytics. Additionally, our platform features a marketplace facilitating direct connections between buyers and farmers, fostering economic growth and efficiency in agricultural trade. A vibrant community forum encourages knowledge sharing among peers, while our informative KrishiTech Blogs offer insights into best practices and industry trends. For farmers seeking enhanced support, we offer a premium subscription service. This subscription includes personalized advice tailored to individual farm needs, access to advanced analytics for optimizing farm operations, and exclusive content that keeps farmers updated on the latest innovations. By leveraging technology and fostering collaboration, KrishiTech is committed to helping farmers maximize productivity, sustainability, and profitability in agriculture.
