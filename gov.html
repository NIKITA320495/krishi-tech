Latest Government Schemes for Farmers

PM-KISAN

The Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN) is a central sector scheme with 100% funding from the Government of India.

How to Apply

Farmers can apply for PM-KISAN through the official website or visit the nearest Common Service Centre (CSC).

Learn More

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) is an insurance service for farmers for their yields.

How to Apply

Farmers can apply for PMFBY through the official website, local agriculture department offices, or authorized insurance agents.

Learn More

Kisan Credit Card (KCC)

The Kisan Credit Card (KCC) scheme aims to provide timely credit to farmers for their cultivation and other needs.

How to Apply

Farmers can apply for a Kisan Credit Card at their nearest bank branch or through the official website of the respective bank.

Learn More

Soil Health Card Scheme

The Soil Health Card Scheme provides farmers with soil nutrient status of their land and advice on soil management practices.

How to Apply

Farmers can apply for the Soil Health Card Scheme through their local agriculture department or via the official website.

Learn More

Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY)

Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY) aims to promote organic farming and improve soil health.

How to Apply

Farmers can join PKVY by contacting their local agriculture department or visiting the official website.

Learn More

Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchai Yojana (PMKSY)

Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchai Yojana (PMKSY) aims to improve farm productivity and ensure better utilization of the resources in the country.

How to Apply

Farmers can apply for PMKSY through their local agriculture department or via the official website.

Learn More

National Agriculture Market (e-NAM)

The e-NAM initiative aims to create a unified national market for agricultural commodities.

How to Apply

Farmers can register on the e-NAM platform through the official website or by visiting the nearest market yard.
